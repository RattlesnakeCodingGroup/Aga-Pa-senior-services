About Aga-Pa

Like the Etymology of the company's name, we believe greatly in protecting seniors and veterans from all harm. We are a start-up company motivated by our passion to care for the residents, in finding a home that provides the highest level of care, and respect for the dignity and individuality of each resident. Aga-pa is locally owned, growing in business and continually adding advocate/team member. We are equipped with 18+ years of hands-on experience caring for residents with different degrees of physical and mental regressions. We know the business inside and out, and understand that residents have different level of care needs, and we match those needs with the appropriate facility.

About the Founder

Years of experiences as a
Professional Administrator and Advocate for facilities

In 2003 two young friends solicited my assistance to purchase their own assisted living facility. I took the mandated CORE class and became the administrator for my young friends and their mentor. Caring for the seniors I experienced a high degree of self-fulfillment for the first time in my career life. 18 years later, I am still high on that feeling and enjoying it.

In my capacity as an administrator, I worked with various sizes of facilities, I met numerous wonderful residents and staff many have remained friends. Unfortunately, I also experienced the bad side of the business. I witnessed abuse, neglect, and exploitation of the seniors committed mostly by families and friends. I was appalled by the gross indifference and total disregard of the lawmakers I reported these crimes to. It is this reason that motivated me to establish my own company. I am determined to continue advocating for the seniors in hope that one day what I do might help prevent or end such crimes.

"Partner in Care" Certified by Alzheimer's Foundation of America (AFA).
We speak English, Tagalog, and Spanish. "SE HABLA ESPANOL"
